Join the tiles to get the 2048 tile!

Use arrow keys or swipe to move

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe to move all tiles
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one
  • After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random spot
  • The game ends when the board is full and no moves are possible
  • Keep playing after reaching 2048 - there's no limit!
